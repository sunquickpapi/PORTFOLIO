DearSkin Application

A personalized skincare companion that simplifies your beauty routine through product tracking, daily habit logging, and visual progress monitoring.

My Role: Mobile App Developer

The Challenge

Maintaining a consistent and effective skincare routine is often overwhelming due to a lack of guided pacing and reminders. Users frequently face Product Confusion regarding layering order and expiration dates, while Subjective Progress makes it difficult to see gradual changes, often leading to the false belief that products are ineffective. Additionally, many traditional apps lack essential Accessibility Features, making them difficult to use for those who prefer hands-free interaction during their actual skincare routine.

The Solution

DearSkin serves as a personalized digital hub that simplifies skincare through Guided Routines & Layering. I developed a smart layering guide that automatically splits routines into Morning (Protect & Prep) and Evening (Repair & Restore), ordering products from thinnest to thickest consistency to ensure maximum absorption. A built-in routine timer further assists users by providing timed execution for specific steps, such as a 60-second cleanser countdown, ensuring products are applied for the optimal duration.

The platform also features a comprehensive Digital Shelf for inventory management, allowing users to track their entire product library and get reminders for expiration dates. This is paired with an AI-Powered Progress Tracking system where users log photos to a Skin Diary for visual analysis. The app provides a detailed metrics breakdown for acne, pores, and texture, ensuring that progress is documented with objective data rather than guesswork.

🏆

Results & Impact

The platform delivers Quantifiable Improvement, allowing users to see exact percentage-based changes in their skin health and make data-backed adjustments to their routines. This transparency has significantly Enhanced Habit Building, as the gamified dashboard tracks active days and completed routines to encourage consistency. By effectively reducing product waste through expiration tracking and increasing user confidence via side-by-side comparisons, DearSkin transforms skincare from a chore into a structured, rewarding journey.

💡

Key Learnings

This project highlighted that Data Validates Effort; providing users with specific regression metrics, like a 16% reduction in acne, provides the vital motivation needed to maintain a routine. It also emphasized that Hands-Free Utility is Essential for skincare apps, as voice control and automated timers allow the app to be a tool rather than a distraction. Finally, I learned that simplifying complex tasks through an automated layering guide is key to long-term user retention, proving that holistic tracking is just as much about building habits as it is about tracking metrics.
