PPASRATUHUB Application

Official mobile application for facility booking, event management, book loan tracking with due dates, book search functionality, and AI-powered book quizzes built with native SwiftUI.

My Role: Full-Stack Developer & Database Architect
Download on the App Store

The Challenge

Prior to this application, users relied on fragmented platforms and manual, in-person processes to access library services. Managing facility bookings, tracking borrowed books, and registering for events created a "hassle" for users, indicating a critical need to consolidate these disjointed workflows into a convenient, digital solution.

The Solution

Developed PPASRATUHUB, a seamless and user-friendly mobile application that centralizes all PPAS services. This "mobile-first" solution digitizes the experience, allowing users to instantly book events, reserve facilities (like study rooms), and track borrowing history and due dates from a single interface, effectively replacing previous manual methods.

🏆

Results & Impact

Successfully achieved digital transformation for PPAS by making library access more convenient and inclusive. The app eliminated manual booking friction for students and researchers and improved account management via automated reminders to avoid late fees. It established a smooth, reliable user experience that consolidates multiple operational needs into one tool.

💡

Key Learnings

Demonstrated the power of collaborative innovation, proving that a dedicated intern team could drive professional-grade digital solutions. The project highlighted the value of integrating fresh ideas with technical skills to solve institutional challenges and emphasized the importance of user-centric design in modernizing public library services.
